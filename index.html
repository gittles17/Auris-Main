How It Works

Think of this as our own private version of ChatGPT with the same interface simplicity, but purpose built for our internal workflows. Auris is your silent partner who handles the details that slow you down.

Open Source Models

We use open source AI models instead of public tools like Gemini or ChatGPT. These are installed on our own hardware so no external provider processes our data and access is fully controlled and auditable.

Library Card Approach

Think of the AI as a smart professor who knows everything except Create. We give that professor a library card to our private archives including Notion, FileMaker, server logs, Adobe, and QuickBooks so they can provide answers while your data stays secure in our controlled environment.

Source Verification

Auris finds the receipts in our actual data. You can always verify its sources. It acts as a drafting assistant where humans always verify and approve.

Bringing Everyone Along

We want Auris to feel like a helpful assistant that makes your day better rather than a corporate mandate. Our plan focuses on curiosity and helping everyone find their own wins.

We will find the natural tech lovers in Editorial, Games, and Design to be our local experts. They will be there to help their teammates find shortcuts and solve specific problems in real time.

FAQ

Why not just use ChatGPT Enterprise or Copilot?

→

Commercial tools do not natively connect across our internal stack in a single query. On-premise hosting also ensures our most sensitive client IP stays in our environment, not on a third party cloud.

Does this replace FileMaker or make it easier to use?

→

We know FileMaker can be clunky and requires repetitive clicking. Auris acts as a bridge. You can ask it for info in plain English rather than navigating multiple menus. It can also push data into FM automatically, sparing you from manual data entry.

Can it help eliminate Double Entry and manual re-typing?

→

Yes. One of its primary goals is to automate the relay of information between departments. It can take info from a design brief and automatically update a finishing tracker or a project slate so you do not have to type the same thing three times.

How does this solve the Information Silo problem between departments?

→

It creates a central intelligence layer. Instead of checking Notion for design, FileMaker for finance, and Teams for status, you can ask Auris for a holistic project overview. It sees across the silos to give you a single source of truth.

Will this support the tools we already use, like Notion, Frame.io, or FileMaker?

→

Absolutely. Auris is built to index data from the platforms you actually use daily. It does not replace your favorite tools, it makes them smarter by connecting their data to the rest of the company.

What about employee privacy?

→

The system references work metadata like project activity, not personal behavior. All implementation will be reviewed with HR and IT to ensure clear boundaries.

Is this replacing our team?

→

No. It is a force multiplier. It handles the boring tasks so our talent can focus on the creative work clients pay us for.

What are the ongoing costs?

→

Modest. We avoid per seat subscription taxes. Costs include electricity, IT oversight, and periodic hardware refreshes.
